Tentukan nilai $a$ dan $b$ pada translasi $\begin{pmatrix}a\\-8\end{pmatrix} + \begin{pmatrix}-5\\2b\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}3\\-2\end{pmatrix}!$
$\begin{pmatrix}a\\-8\end{pmatrix} + \begin{pmatrix}-5\\2b\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}3\\-2\end{pmatrix}$

  • $\begin{align}=> a + (-5) &= 3 \\ a &= 3 + 5 \\ a &= 8 \\ \end{align}$
  • $\begin{align} => -8 + 2b &= -2 \\ 2b &= -2 + 8 \\ 2b &= 6\\ b &= \frac{6}{2}\\ b &= 3 \\ \end{align}$

Jadi, nilai $a = 8$ dan $b = 3.$
